Privacy Policy for POV

Last updated: May 2026

Data Collection

POV collects and stores:

  • Video clips you record
  • Audio within videos
  • Written reflections and answers
  • Mood and director selections
  • Timestamps and entry metadata

All data is stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

  • All data is stored privately on your iPhone
  • We do not have access to your data
  • Data is encrypted by iOS
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No data is sent to our servers
  • No accounts or registration required

How We Use Your Data

  • Video clips are assembled into films using on-device processing
  • Reflections are analyzed locally (on your phone) to identify patterns
  • Insights are generated and stored locally
  • No data leaves your device

Permissions Explained

Camera:

  • Used only when you record video
  • Videos are stored locally on your device

Microphone:

  • Used only to record audio within videos
  • Audio is stored locally as part of your video file

Notifications (Optional):

  • One soft reminder at 10pm to reflect
  • Only if you enable it in settings
  • No tracking of whether you read it

Third-Party Services

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No ads
  • No data sharing with third parties
  • No remote processing of your data

Your Rights

  • You own all your data
  • You can access your videos anytime
  • You can delete individual entries or the entire app at any time
  • Data is permanently deleted when you uninstall POV

Data Retention

  • Video clips: Stored until you delete them
  • Reflections: Stored until you delete them
  • Insight cards: Stored until you delete them
  • App data: All deleted when you uninstall POV
  • No backups stored on our servers

Children's Privacy

POV is not intended for children under 13. If we discover a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
